  title: What is Leadview.io?
  description:
  - Leadview is a lead identification platform that shows sales and marketing teams
    exactly which companies are visiting their website. It sits within the sales intelligence
    category, converting anonymous web traffic into named prospects with contact details
    for key decision-makers. The core advantage is the ability to act on warm interest
    that would otherwise go unnoticed, targeting companies that have already demonstrated
    intent by visiting the site.
  - 'Getting started with Leadview involves three steps: booking a demo, adding a
    short code snippet to the site, and beginning to capture leads. Identified companies
    and contacts become visible as soon as the tracker is active, giving teams immediate
    visibility into who is engaging with their content. The platform is particularly
    useful for generating pipeline from inbound traffic without waiting for visitors
    to fill out a form.'
  - Leadview connects with widely used CRM platforms, enriching existing sales records
    and supporting workflow automation. It is not a full marketing automation suite
    or email campaign manager. Its scope is focused on visitor identification and
    contact delivery, making it a strong complementary tool for teams that already
    have outreach infrastructure in place and want sharper lead intelligence.
