  title: What is LeadGraph?
  description:
  - LeadGraph is a market intelligence platform built for professionals who need comprehensive,
    verified data on companies worldwide. Its knowledge graph covers 245 million global
    organizations, including 79,000 public companies and 51,000 venture-backed startups,
    all structured and maintained by AI that continuously processes data from a wide
    range of public sources. This breadth and depth make it a strong choice for due
    diligence, competitive research, and identifying new market opportunities.
  - The platform delivers actionable output quickly. Users can generate ready-to-download
    reports and data charts covering revenue history, funding rounds, known customers,
    and competitors within minutes of initiating a search. LeadGraph's news tracking
    is particularly broad, indexing sources that many competing tools overlook, which
    helps users stay current on market developments before they become widely reported.
  - LeadGraph connects to CRM, sales, and enrichment workflows through an API, fitting
    naturally into existing GTM tech stacks. It is not intended for contact management
    or mass email campaigns. Its value lies in providing verified, in-depth company
    intelligence to analysts, sales teams, and business development professionals
    who need accurate data to support faster, better-informed decisions.
