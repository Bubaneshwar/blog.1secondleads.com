  title: What is Leadbright?
  description:
  - Leadbright LOOP is an AI-powered lead generation and revenue growth platform built
    for B2B companies. It applies machine learning and big data to analyze your website
    alongside competitor sites, surfacing clear SEO and marketing insights without
    requiring weeks of manual analysis. By automating the tasks that flow from those
    insights, it reduces the time and cost traditionally associated with digital growth
    programs.
  - The platform is most effective for teams focused on organic lead generation, improving
    website conversion rates, and staying current with industry trends. LOOP runs
    continuously in the background, optimizing your digital presence with minimal
    ongoing input, which makes it practical for both lean startups and larger organizations
    that want efficient, scalable growth.
  - Within a marketing technology stack, LOOP acts as a growth and analytics engine
    that feeds quality leads into CRMs and email platforms downstream. It is not designed
    for direct sales outreach or CRM management. Its purpose is to simplify the data
    analysis and lead capture work that amplifies the impact of those other tools.
