what_is:
  title: What is MailboxValidator?
  description:
  - MailboxValidator is an email verification service that checks whether addresses
    are valid and actively receiving mail. It does this by checking syntax, MX records,
    and live server connections, then returning results in JSON or XML. Users can
    upload bulk lists in text or CSV format, making it practical for teams managing
    large contact databases.
  - Beyond basic validation, the tool flags disposable addresses, free-provider accounts,
    role-based emails, and spam traps, and it removes duplicate entries in the same
    pass. A free plan with 100 credits lets teams evaluate accuracy before committing,
    and both single and bulk validation modes are supported for flexible workflows.
  - MailboxValidator integrates with platforms such as Mailchimp and SendGrid, fitting
    cleanly into an existing email marketing stack. It is a dedicated list-hygiene
    tool rather than a full email marketing or CRM solution, so teams use it as a
    pre-send quality layer that protects deliverability and keeps campaign costs in
    check.
