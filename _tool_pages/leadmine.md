  - 'LeadMine is a lead generation tool built to help businesses find accurate B2B
    contacts alongside verified email addresses. It draws on a database of over 200
    million business contacts and targets 95% email deliverability, giving users confidence
    that the data they export will actually reach the inbox. Getting started costs
    nothing: a free tier provides 10 monthly credits with no credit card required,
    and search filters covering location, industry, and company attributes let users
    build tightly targeted prospect lists.'
  - The platform also supports fast email discovery by name and domain, making it
    practical for quickly verifying whether a specific contact is reachable before
    reaching out. Leads are available immediately after a search completes, which
    speeds up sales outreach cycles. Within a go-to-market stack, LeadMine functions
    primarily as a data enrichment and email discovery layer, accessible through a
    web dashboard and a Chrome extension that allows exports at any point in the workflow.
  - LeadMine is not built for managing CRM records or running email campaigns, so
    teams will need separate tools to handle those functions. Its value lies specifically
    in precise lead finding and email verification, giving sales pipelines a steady
    supply of accurate, deliverable contact data.
