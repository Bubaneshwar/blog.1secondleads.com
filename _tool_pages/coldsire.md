what_is:
  title: What is ColdSire?
  description:
  - ColdSire is a premium cold email infrastructure tool that improves deliverability
    by providing genuine Google and Microsoft mailboxes rather than synthetic accounts.
    It belongs to the cold email infrastructure category and helps businesses avoid
    spam issues by mixing cold-email accounts with active, legitimate business accounts
    in its pool. A hard cap of 15% on cold email activity within that pool ensures
    individual senders are not flagged or banned.
  - The service suits agencies and companies running high-volume cold email campaigns
    that require real domain isolation and reliable inbox placement. Account setup
    averages just five minutes, and SPF, DKIM, and DMARC records are configured automatically,
    so users can begin sending within the same day.
  - ColdSire integrates directly with outreach platforms such as Instantly.ai and
    SmartLead.ai, serving as the deliverability layer in a broader go-to-market stack.
    It is not a CRM, a data enrichment tool, or a cold email sequencing platform;
    its sole purpose is supplying clean, properly authenticated mailboxes for sending
    cold email safely.
