  title: What is FullContact?
  description:
  - FullContact is an identity resolution platform built to identify website visitors,
    map their journeys, and convert anonymous traffic into actionable leads. It sits
    within the customer intelligence and data enrichment category. The platform differentiates
    itself by consolidating fragmented data points into unified customer records and
    appending more than 900 unique attributes for deeper audience understanding.
  - Teams typically see value within days, as the enriched profiles can immediately
    inform personalized outreach and more precise ad targeting. FullContact is well
    suited to marketing, sales, and product teams that need a complete picture of
    their customers across touchpoints. It connects with CRMs, CDPs, and marketing
    platforms to strengthen first-party data and extend media reach.
  - FullContact is not a standalone CRM or email marketing solution. Its core function
    is identity resolution and data enrichment, designed to improve the tools already
    in place rather than replace them.
