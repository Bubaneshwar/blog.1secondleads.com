  title: What is Hyperbound?
  description:
  - Hyperbound is an AI sales coaching tool built around roleplay. Reps practice cold,
    warm, and discovery calls against realistic AI buyers and get actionable feedback,
    which scales coaching that would otherwise need a manager on every call.
  - It is strongest for onboarding new hires, practicing call types, and certifying
    reps on new messaging. Practice and feedback are available immediately without
    a live prospect, and its AI analyzes real calls to find skill gaps and recommend
    training.
  - Hyperbound integrates with call systems and CRMs to import calls and deliver scorecards.

    through practice and coaching.
