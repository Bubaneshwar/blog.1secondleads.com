what_is:
  title: What is LeadDelta?
  description:
  - LeadDelta is a LinkedIn CRM built to help individuals and teams manage and enrich
    their professional connections. It makes it easy to search across your entire
    network, apply tags to segment contacts, and retrieve verified emails and phone
    numbers without switching between tools. The ability to recall every connection
    and the context around it is what draws users to it, whether they are working
    independently or collaborating within a team.
  - LeadDelta is most valuable for CEOs, sales professionals, recruiters, and marketers
    who need their LinkedIn contacts organized and actionable. The platform delivers
    immediate utility after setup, allowing users to begin enriching and segmenting
    their existing connections right away. It serves the CRM and contact data management
    layer of a go-to-market stack, improving the quality of LinkedIn outreach and
    contact records.
  - LeadDelta is not designed for traditional pipeline management or deep marketing
    automation workflows, so teams with those requirements will want to pair it with
    tools built for those purposes. Its strength lies specifically in organizing and
    enriching LinkedIn connection data.
