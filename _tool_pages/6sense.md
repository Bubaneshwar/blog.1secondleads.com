  title: What is 6Sense?
  description:
  - 6sense is a revenue intelligence platform that helps sales and marketing teams
    find and prioritize accounts that are actively researching a purchase. It analyzes
    intent and engagement signals across the web to estimate where each account sits
    in its buying journey, so teams can focus effort where it is most likely to convert.
  - It is built for go-to-market teams running account-based programs across both
    inbound and outbound. Common uses include prioritizing accounts for outreach,
    personalizing email and ads, and targeting competitor or look-alike accounts,
    with AI handling much of the research and timing.
  - 6sense sits alongside your CRM, email, and enrichment tools as the intelligence
    layer rather than a replacement for them. It informs and automates campaigns with
    account-level data, but you still run messaging and pipeline management in your
    existing stack.
