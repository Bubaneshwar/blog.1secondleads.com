what_is:
  title: What is Leadspace?
  description:
  - Leadspace is a B2B data platform built for sales and marketing teams that need
    to find, prioritize, and engage the right accounts faster. It combines data from
    more than 30 third-party providers with a company's own first-party records to
    give a clear picture of the Total Addressable Market and the Ideal Customer Profile.
    With coverage of more than 700 million contacts and 200 million companies, plus
    account hierarchy mapping and buying signal scoring, it provides the depth of
    data needed for precise lead matching and segmentation.
  - The platform is particularly strong when teams want to improve targeting accuracy,
    sharpen campaign segmentation, and align sales engagement around the best opportunities.
    Market exploration and ICP modeling are available quickly, as the enrichment and
    scoring capabilities work against integrated data to surface high-confidence account
    and contact recommendations. This accelerates demand generation planning and territory
    assignment by grounding both in current, enriched data rather than assumptions.
  - Leadspace integrates with CRM and marketing automation platforms, serving as a
    data enrichment, segmentation, and activation layer within the existing go-to-market
    stack. It is not a CRM itself, nor is it a standalone sales outreach tool. It
    is best suited to teams running data-driven account-based marketing and sales
    programs that require robust enrichment and scoring rather than basic contact
    management or cold outreach.
