what_is:
  title: What is LeadGibbon?
  description:
  - LeadGibbon is a B2B lead intelligence platform focused on email discovery and
    verification. Its core feature is a LinkedIn Chrome extension that retrieves a
    validated work email address in a single click while browsing any LinkedIn profile.
    Behind that extension sits a database of more than 26 million records and an integrated
    email verifier, together delivering 97% accuracy so users send to addresses that
    actually exist.
  - The platform is well suited for building targeted lead lists filtered by role,
    industry, or company, and for enriching existing contact lists by uploading CSV
    files for bulk email validation. Because results appear immediately, either through
    the extension or via batch enrichment, teams can move from prospecting to outreach
    without a long data-preparation phase. LeadGibbon integrates with popular CRMs
    and email tools, so verified contacts slot directly into established workflows.
  - LeadGibbon is a focused email finder and verifier rather than a full marketing
    automation or multi-channel campaign management system. It is most valuable as
    a fast, accurate source of verified email addresses within a broader outreach
    stack.
