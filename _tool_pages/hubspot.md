what_is:
  title: What is HubSpot?
  description:
  - HubSpot is an all-in-one customer platform that unites marketing, sales, and service
    teams on a single CRM. It keeps business data in one place so teams can manage
    leads, close deals, and support customers without stitching tools together, with
    built-in AI to speed routine work.
  - It is useful for managing relationships, automating outreach, running campaigns,
    and scaling support. Because the hubs share one data model, teams can start seeing
    value quickly and add capabilities as they grow.
  - HubSpot connects with a very large app marketplace to keep systems unified across
    marketing, sales, service, content, and data. It is not an accounting or heavy
    ERP system; it is built for growth-focused go-to-market teams.
