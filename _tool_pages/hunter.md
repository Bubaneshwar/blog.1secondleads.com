  title: What is Hunter?
  description:
  - Hunter is an email-finding and outreach tool. Give it a domain or a name and it
    returns likely professional addresses, then checks them so your list does not
    bounce. From there you can launch cold email campaigns without moving the data
    into another app.
  - It suits salespeople, recruiters, and marketers who need verified contacts quickly
    and want personalized cold outreach that still feels human. The interface is simple
    and there is a free tier, so a team can go from a domain to a sent campaign in
    minutes.
  - In a wider stack Hunter acts as an enrichment and sending layer, connecting to
    CRMs, Google Sheets, and other tools. It is not a heavy marketing-automation platform;
    its strength is building accurate lists and running targeted cold email.
