  - InStream is a free online CRM designed for sales teams that want a simple, effective
    way to manage customer relationships. It centralizes contact data, gathers insights
    from social media profiles, and organizes communication history to help teams
    build stronger client connections. Users adopt it to create a consistent sales
    culture and improve team coordination through an intuitive dashboard.

    the benefits quickly and improve pipeline predictability without a long ramp-up
    period.
  - InStream integrates with Gmail, LinkedIn, Facebook, and Twitter to enrich contact

    want a straightforward, socially enriched CRM to strengthen sales relationships
