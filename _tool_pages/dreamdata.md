  title: What is Dreamdata?
  description:
  - Dreamdata is a B2B marketing analytics platform focused on attribution. It maps
    the entire customer journey and connects activity to revenue, so marketers can
    see which campaigns and channels are actually working rather than guessing.
  - It is strongest at analyzing all marketing activity, building precise audiences,
    and spotting buying intent early, with ROI reports and interactive journey timelines
    that speed up decisions. It also syncs conversion data back to ad platforms to
    sharpen targeting.
  - Dreamdata connects to ad platforms, CRMs, and tools like Slack and Teams, acting
    as a central data hub for attribution and activation. It is not an email or CRM
    tool; it focuses on journey mapping and revenue analytics.
