what_is:
  title: What is Emailable?
  description:
  - Emailable is an email verification platform built to improve deliverability by
    cleaning and validating email lists before they go to work in campaigns. It sits
    within the email marketing and data validation category, distinguishing itself
    with a 99% deliverability guarantee and the capacity to verify more than 30,000
    addresses per minute. Marketing and sales teams use it to ensure their contact
    data is accurate before hitting send.
  - The platform protects sender reputation by flagging and removing invalid, disposable,
    or high-risk addresses that would otherwise generate bounces or spam complaints.
    Bulk list verification, real-time validation at web form capture points, and ongoing
    inbox placement monitoring are its core capabilities. Teams see cleaner lists
    and improved deliverability almost immediately, which supports faster and safer
    campaign execution.
  - Emailable connects to CRMs, marketing platforms, and other email tools through
    an API or embeddable widgets, and it enriches records with domain and SMTP metadata
    in the process. It does not send emails itself and is not a marketing automation
    platform. It is designed to work alongside existing email infrastructure as the
    validation layer that keeps lists healthy and campaigns out of the spam folder.
