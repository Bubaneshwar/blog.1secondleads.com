what_is:
  title: What is Leadberry?
  description:
  - Leadberry is a web-based B2B lead generation tool that converts anonymous site
    visitors into identified, contactable leads. It leverages existing Google Analytics
    data to determine which companies are browsing your site and then layers in contact
    information and social details for each. Thousands of companies, from early-stage
    startups to Fortune 500 organizations, rely on Leadberry to automate the prospecting
    process without manual research.
  - The platform is particularly useful for sales and marketing teams that want to
    act on inbound interest by identifying qualified visitors, reviewing their on-site
    behavior, and filtering records against custom criteria. Setup involves adding
    a lightweight tracking snippet to the site, after which lead data appears almost
    immediately. The absence of a credit card requirement for signup lowers the barrier
    to evaluating it quickly.
  - Leadberry connects to popular CRMs, email platforms, and sales tools so leads
    flow directly into existing workflows with configurable alerts. It is designed
    strictly for B2B visitor identification and is not intended for consumer lead
    generation or marketing automation. Its value is focused on enriching website
    traffic data to fuel targeted outreach.
