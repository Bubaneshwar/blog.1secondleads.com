what_is:
  title: What is Leadinfo?
  description:
  - Leadinfo is a B2B lead generation tool that identifies companies visiting your
    website in real time, converting anonymous traffic into named sales opportunities.
    When a company lands on your site, Leadinfo surfaces its details alongside the
    contact information of relevant decision-makers, drawing on a database of 220
    million companies across 195 countries. The platform is trusted by over 15,000
    users and uses AI to maintain accuracy while keeping all data GDPR-compliant.
  - The tool is particularly useful for sales and marketing teams that want to prioritize
    outreach based on demonstrated buying intent. Because Leadinfo shows which companies
    are actively visiting your site, teams can focus on warm prospects rather than
    cold lists, shortening the path from visitor to conversation. Qualified leads
    can be identified and added to outreach campaigns within weeks of setup.
  - Leadinfo integrates with more than 70 platforms, including CRMs, email tools,
    and LinkedIn, so enriched visitor data can flow directly into existing workflows
    for follow-up and nurturing. The platform is designed for B2B lead identification
    rather than B2C use cases or deep behavioral analytics, and its focus remains
    on connecting businesses with the companies and decision-makers most likely to
    be interested in their offering.
