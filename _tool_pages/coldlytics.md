what_is:
  title: What is Coldlytics?
  description:
  - Coldlytics is a lead generation service built for agencies and businesses that
    need targeted, verified contact lists. It combines automation, AI, and human researchers
    to build accurate prospect lists from scratch rather than pulling from a static
    database. Plans include 25 free leads per month, with paid tiers offering up to
    1,000 leads and rollover credits. Human-verified data and a bounce-back refund
    policy set it apart from fully automated alternatives.
  - Coldlytics is especially well suited to agencies and freelancers looking for fresh,
    niche-specific B2B leads in English-speaking markets such as the US, UK, Canada,
    and Australia. Once a search is submitted, custom lists are delivered within 24
    hours, making it practical for ongoing outreach and quick campaign starts.
  - It fits into sales stacks as a data enrichment and prospecting input, supplying
    clean contact data to CRMs and email outreach tools. Because it is an on-demand
    research service rather than an instant-access database, it is not the right fit
    for bulk data scraping or situations requiring immediate results. Its advantage
    is data quality over raw volume.
