what_is:
  title: What is Coldreach?
  description:
  - Coldreach is a sales automation tool built for outbound lead generation and engagement.
    It uses AI to monitor more than 79 million accounts 24/7, identifying buying signals
    that are fully customizable to match the specific intent criteria for a given
    product. This continuous monitoring lets sales teams reach the right accounts
    at the right time rather than guessing who is in market.
  - The platform is most effective when teams need to build targeted lead lists quickly
    and want personalized outreach to run at scale. Lead lists are refreshed weekly,
    and AI-powered outreach to high-intent accounts begins immediately. Sales teams
    using Coldreach typically book more meetings by sending tailored emails tied to
    real-time account activity rather than generic sequences.
  - Coldreach connects with CRMs, email platforms, and engagement tools including
    Salesforce, HubSpot, and Sales Navigator, fitting naturally into an existing tech
    stack. It is not a full CRM or pipeline management platform; its focus is exclusively
    on prospecting and outreach automation, so separate tools remain necessary for
    deal tracking and sales reporting.
