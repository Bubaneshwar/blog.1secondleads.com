  title: What is Mailcheck?
  description:
  - Mailcheck is an email verification service that cleans and validates mailing lists
    to ensure every address belongs to a real, active user. It uses social network
    signals and proprietary algorithms to filter out bots and dormant accounts, with
    the aim of increasing email delivery rates by up to five times. A 40-day money-back
    guarantee gives teams a risk-free way to evaluate the service.
  - For teams running high-volume campaigns, Mailcheck processes up to one million
    emails in roughly 15 minutes, delivering results quickly enough to fit into fast-moving
    outreach schedules. Marketers, sales teams, and e-commerce operators benefit most
    from the speed and accuracy, saving time and budget that would otherwise be wasted
    on undeliverable contacts.
  - The tool connects to marketing platforms via API, making it straightforward to
    incorporate into existing workflows as a data-quality layer. It is not a campaign
    management or bulk-sending platform; its purpose is to verify and clean lists
    before outreach begins, so teams launch every campaign with the highest-quality
    data available.
