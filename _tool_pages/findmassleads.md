what_is:
  title: What is Findmassleads?
  description:
  - Findmassleads is a web lead generation tool aimed at small businesses, freelancers,
    and agencies that want to identify and reach potential clients based on the technology
    those clients use on their websites. Instead of relying on a static general-purpose
    database, it queries live web data and filters prospects by tech stack, giving
    users a targeted and current pool of leads to work from.
  - The tool is particularly effective when a seller needs to quickly pinpoint potential
    clients running specific platforms, such as Shopify, Magento, or Facebook Pixel,
    because that technology use is itself a buying signal. Searches return results
    within minutes, so users can move into outreach without lengthy data preparation.
    Leads can be exported via CSV or pulled through an API, making it straightforward
    to push contacts into a CRM or cold email platform.
  - Findmassleads is not an email marketing or campaign automation platform and does
    not send messages on its own. Its function is solely to surface fresh, relevant
    lead data based on technology criteria, leaving campaign execution to whatever
    outreach tools are already in a user's stack.
