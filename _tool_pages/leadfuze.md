what_is:
  title: What is LeadFuze?
  description:
  - LeadFuze is a lead generation platform built for sales, marketing, and recruiting
    professionals who need accurate, targeted contact data at scale. It provides access
    to more than 300 million verified professional profiles, each carrying detailed
    information such as email addresses, phone numbers, and addresses. The database
    is refreshed monthly, which keeps the data current and reduces the time teams
    spend chasing bounced contacts or outdated records.
  - Where LeadFuze stands out is in how quickly users can move from search to a ready-to-use
    prospect list. Hyper-targeted search filters let you define exactly who you want
    to reach, and the built-in automation handles list assembly without manual effort.
    Sales pipelines, marketing campaigns, and candidate sourcing workflows all benefit
    from this speed, allowing outreach to begin almost immediately after setting up
    a search.
  - LeadFuze is designed to serve as the data foundation in an existing go-to-market
    stack. It integrates with CRMs and email platforms so enriched contacts flow directly
    into the tools teams already use for outreach and follow-up. It is not a CRM or
    email marketing platform itself, making it the right fit for teams that have outreach
    infrastructure in place and need reliable, clean prospect data to power it.
