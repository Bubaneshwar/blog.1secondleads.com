what_is:
  title: What is Enrichley?
  description:
  - Enrichley is an email verification and data enrichment platform serving marketers
    and sales teams that deal with hard-to-verify contact lists. Its core differentiation
    is its ability to validate addresses on catch-all domains, including those shielded
    by Secure Email Gateways, surfacing 20 to 30 percent more genuine contacts than
    tools that simply mark catch-all addresses as undeliverable. This directly reduces
    bounce rates and protects sender reputation for teams running outbound programs.
  - Beyond verification, Enrichley enriches contact profiles with more than 50 data
    points, covering work history, company information, and other attributes that
    make outreach more targeted and relevant. Results are returned quickly, allowing
    teams to recover real opportunities from lists they would otherwise discard and
    to add context that improves personalization. The platform fits into a CRM or
    email workflow as an enrichment and hygiene layer that raises both accuracy and
    engagement.
  - Enrichley is not a bulk data scraping or lead generation service. Its role is
    verification and enrichment of contacts already in your possession, making outreach
    smarter rather than expanding the raw size of a list. Teams that invest in contact
    quality over contact quantity will find it a focused and effective complement
    to their existing sales and marketing tools.
