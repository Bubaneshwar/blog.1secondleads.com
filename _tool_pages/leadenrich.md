  description:
  - LeadEnrich is an AI-powered lead enrichment tool built specifically for real estate
    professionals. It takes incomplete owner data and resolves it into accurate, actionable
    contact records, covering phone numbers and other details needed for outreach.
    With a reported 95% average match rate and design choices that keep it compliant
    with TCPA and FCRA regulations, it is built for reliable and legally sound prospecting.
  - The platform is most useful when speed and data accuracy matter, such as rapid
    prospecting or following up on time-sensitive leads. Users upload their lists
    in CSV, Excel, or TXT format and receive enriched contact information within seconds.
    Wholesalers, agents, and brokers who want to reduce research time and increase
    the proportion of contactable leads in their pipelines are the primary audience.
  - LeadEnrich connects with CRMs and supports multi-format data exports for easy
    handoff to downstream tools. It also includes AI-driven data cleaning and an AI
    chat assistant for querying results. The platform is not a property listing service
    or direct marketing tool; its scope is skip tracing and lead enrichment for real
    estate outreach.
