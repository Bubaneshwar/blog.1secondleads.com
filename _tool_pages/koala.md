what_is:
  title: What is Koala?
  description:
  - Koala is a signal and intent tool that helps sales and RevOps teams work the right
    accounts. It identifies the companies behind website traffic and pulls signals
    from many sources into a single view, so reps can see who is showing interest
    and act on it.
  - It is most useful for sales development and marketing teams that want to prioritize
    high-intent accounts and build a repeatable pipeline. Setup is quick, and account
    scoring plus a clear view of the buying committee make outreach more timely and
    relevant.
  - Koala integrates with CRMs, email platforms, and data warehouses to automate enrichment
    and keep records current. It is not a CRM or marketing-automation platform itself;
    it aggregates intent and helps reps prioritize and engage.
