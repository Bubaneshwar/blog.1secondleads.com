  title: What is Emailchaser?
  description:
  - Emailchaser is a cold email outreach platform built for sales teams, agencies,
    and go-to-market specialists. Its flat-rate pricing model covers unlimited email
    accounts, leads, and seats, removing the scaling friction that per-seat pricing
    creates. The core use cases are booking meetings, managing multiple sending accounts,
    and running automated multi-step follow-up sequences that land in the primary
    inbox.
  - AI-driven personalization helps tailor messages to each prospect, improving response
    rates over generic templates. Inbox rotation distributes sending load across accounts
    to maintain strong deliverability and keep emails out of spam folders. Emailchaser
    functions as both an outreach platform and a lightweight sales CRM within the
    go-to-market stack, and it connects with Slack, HubSpot, Salesforce, and other
    tools for workflow continuity.
  - The platform is built for outbound cold email campaigns and lead engagement rather
    than inbound CRM workflows or full marketing automation. Teams that need a cost-effective
    way to run high-volume personalized outreach across multiple accounts will find
    it a practical fit, while those requiring advanced inbound pipeline management
    will need a dedicated CRM alongside it.
