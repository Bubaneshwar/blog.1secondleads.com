  description:
  - Lead Liaison is a sales and marketing automation platform designed to help businesses
    increase revenue by managing the full lead lifecycle from initial attraction through
    conversion and retention. It tracks website visitors in detail, automates marketing
    tasks, and manages leads across channels, with standout features including deep
    visitor identification, custom forms, and real-time lead segmentation. Dedicated
    onboarding support and a certification program help teams get up to speed quickly.
  - The platform performs particularly well in event settings, where its onsite lead
    capture tools enable sales teams to collect and qualify prospects in real time.
    Custom forms and automated segmentation mean that leads can be routed and engaged
    immediately after capture, compressing the time between first contact and a qualified
    conversation. Lead Liaison integrates with CRMs and email platforms, positioning
    it as a central hub for all lead management and marketing insight activity.
  - Lead Liaison is not a simple standalone CRM or a basic email tool. It is built
    for companies that need a combined sales and marketing solution with advanced
    tracking, automation, and analytics in a single platform, making it most valuable
    to B2B teams running both event-based and digital demand generation programs.
