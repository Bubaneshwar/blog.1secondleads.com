what_is:
  title: What is Mailscale?
  description:
  - Mailscale is an inbox provisioning service built for agencies and B2B firms that
    run cold email outreach at volume. It automates the creation of multiple company
    email accounts, handling all DNS, DKIM, and SPF record setup so teams do not need
    to configure each account manually. The service can generate more than 50 inboxes
    in under a minute and reduces infrastructure costs by up to 80 percent compared
    to standard mailbox providers.
  - The primary use case is scaling personalized outreach to large prospect lists
    without running into deliverability problems caused by sending too much volume
    from too few accounts. Businesses using Mailscale to contact thousands of prospects
    each month report growth in booked appointments and inbound leads, with accounts
    ready to use immediately after provisioning.
  - Mailscale works as the inbox layer in a cold outreach stack, pairing with sending
    platforms such as Instantly or Smartlead. It is an infrastructure provider, not
    a sending tool, and it is not suited for transactional email, newsletters, or
    subscriber-based communication. Its value is specifically in giving outbound teams
    the volume of high-deliverability inboxes they need to reach new prospects consistently.
