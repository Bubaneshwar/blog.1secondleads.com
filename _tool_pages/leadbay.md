what_is:
  title: What is Leadbay?
  description:
  - Leadbay is a lead discovery and qualification platform designed for small and
    medium-sized businesses. It helps sales teams uncover prospects that are difficult
    to surface through conventional tools, making it easier to expand pipelines with
    genuinely new opportunities. Backed by Y Combinator, the product is built around
    finding unique SMB leads, though it is not yet available in all markets and prospective
    users in unsupported regions can join a waiting list.
  - Sales and marketing teams looking to broaden their lead lists with hard-to-find
    contacts benefit most from Leadbay. New leads become available shortly after onboarding,
    and the platform's qualification layer helps teams prioritize the most promising
    prospects rather than sorting through raw lists manually. It sits within the go-to-market
    stack as a lead enrichment and discovery layer.
  - Leadbay pairs well with CRMs and email marketing tools by feeding them fresh,
    qualified lead data. Its current focus on SMBs and selective country availability
    means it is not yet a fit for large enterprises or teams that require global coverage,
    so verifying regional support before adopting is worthwhile.
