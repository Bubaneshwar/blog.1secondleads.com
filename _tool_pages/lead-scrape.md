what_is:
  title: What is Lead Scrape?
  description:
  - Lead Scrape is a straightforward lead generation tool built to help businesses
    find B2B contacts quickly and at low cost. By entering a keyword and a target
    location, users can retrieve company profiles, email addresses, phone numbers,
    and decision-maker contacts covering more than 50 countries. The approach is designed
    to replace costly advertising, stale purchased lists, and time-consuming manual
    research with an automated process that surfaces thousands of potential customers
    in minutes.
  - The tool is well suited to building sales funnels rapidly, particularly for marketers
    and sales teams who need quality prospect lists for email outreach or social media
    campaigns. Because results appear immediately after a search runs, teams can move
    from data collection to outreach in the same session. There is no complex setup
    required, making it accessible for businesses at various stages of building their
    outbound process.
  - 'Lead Scrape connects with popular marketing and sales tools including HubSpot
    and Lemlist, allowing users to feed discovered contacts directly into CRM records
    and email sequences. It is not designed for deep market analysis or for sourcing
    non-B2B leads. Its role in a go-to-market stack is specific: fast, cost-effective
    lead discovery to fuel B2B sales prospecting and outreach efforts.'
