what_is:
  title: What is LeadsFactory?
  description:
  - 'LeadsFactory is a lead generation tool built to identify the right contacts within
    target companies by scraping Sales Navigator live, with no LinkedIn account required
    on the user''s side. Its core capability is smart waterfall persona search: users
    define a target job title and a set of fallback criteria, and the tool matches
    the closest available title in each company. This makes it practical for finding
    decision-makers such as CMOs or Heads of Growth even when organizations use unconventional
    role names.'
  - The workflow starts by uploading a list of target companies and configuring persona
    rules with fallback logic. LeadsFactory then scrapes live Sales Navigator data
    to return the best-matched contacts for each account. Because the data is pulled
    in real time rather than from a static database, the contacts returned reflect
    current organizational structures, and results are available shortly after the
    search is configured.
  - LeadsFactory fits into a go-to-market stack as a data enrichment and lead sourcing
    layer that feeds accurate, persona-matched contacts into CRM systems and sales
    outreach tools. It is not designed for bulk data management or end-to-end workflow
    automation; its focus is precise, real-time people finding based on defined persona
    criteria.
