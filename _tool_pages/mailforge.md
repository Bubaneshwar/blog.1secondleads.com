what_is:
  title: What is Mailforge?
  description:
  - Mailforge is a cold email infrastructure platform designed to help businesses
    of all sizes launch and manage multi-domain outreach operations. It automates
    DNS configuration, supports unlimited mailboxes, and includes SSL and domain masking,
    all built to maximize inbox placement. The platform is positioned as a cost-efficient
    alternative to provisioning mailboxes through Google Workspace or Microsoft 365.
  - Teams can have a fully configured cold outreach infrastructure ready in five minutes,
    making it practical to scale quickly without deep technical knowledge. The distributed
    email architecture reduces the risk of deliverability problems that arise when
    sending volume is concentrated on too few domains or IP addresses. Both new users
    and experienced operators find the setup process straightforward.
  - Mailforge works alongside any cold email sending software and pairs well with
    CRM tools to support a complete outbound sales pipeline. For teams that need mainstream
    Google or Microsoft mailbox integration rather than dedicated cold-outreach infrastructure,
    Primeforge is the companion product. Mailforge itself is built specifically for
    scaling cold outreach, not transactional or subscriber-based email sending.
