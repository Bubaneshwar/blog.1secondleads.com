  description:
  - EcomLeads is a lead generation platform designed for ecommerce businesses and
    marketers who need to reach verified decision-makers quickly. Its database covers
    more than 371,000 contacts and 42,000 companies across 160 countries, and every
    record is GDPR compliant. Beyond contact details, the platform layers in real-time
    website analytics to give users richer context on each prospect before they reach
    out.
  - The platform is well suited for launching targeted marketing campaigns, building
    distribution or supplier partnerships, and improving sales outreach efficiency.
    Qualified leads are available immediately after signup, and each record includes
    verified email addresses alongside company data such as revenue figures and technology
    stack. Lead scoring is on the roadmap, which will add an additional layer of prioritization
    for outreach teams.
  - EcomLeads exports contacts to CRMs and email marketing platforms, making it easy
    to incorporate into an existing go-to-market stack. The tool is purpose-built
    for ecommerce contact discovery and enrichment rather than broad market research
    or CRM management. Teams that need a focused, accurate database of ecommerce contacts
    will find it a practical fit.
