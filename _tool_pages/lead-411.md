  title: What is Lead 411?
  description:
  - Lead411 is a B2B lead generation and sales intelligence platform built to help
    sales teams and marketers find current, verified contact information at scale.
    It belongs to the sales intelligence and lead data category, and its standout
    feature is unlimited search and export access starting at $49 per month, paired
    with verified emails and direct dials covering millions of companies.
  - The platform is well suited for identifying decision-makers, targeting accounts
    that show growth or active hiring signals, and enriching existing lead records
    to accelerate pipeline building. Real-time data and intent signals mean teams
    can take action immediately after setup rather than waiting for data to populate.
    Lead411 connects with popular CRMs such as Salesforce and HubSpot, as well as
    outreach platforms like Outreach.io, so prospecting and lead management stay in
    sync.
  - Lead411 is focused on B2B data and sales intelligence and is not designed for
    consumer lead targeting or marketing automation. Teams that need broader campaign
    execution tools will want to pair it with a dedicated marketing platform, using
    Lead411 to supply the high-quality contact data those tools require.
