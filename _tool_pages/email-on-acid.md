what_is:
  title: What is Email on Acid?
  description:
  - Email on Acid is an email testing and optimization platform designed for marketers
    who need certainty that their campaigns render correctly before deployment. It
    falls within the email marketing toolset and stands out by offering unlimited
    previews across more than 100 devices and email clients, including dark mode environments.
    This breadth of coverage ensures that a message looks as intended for every recipient,
    regardless of what app or device they use to open it.
  - The platform is most valuable when flawless rendering and inbox placement are
    non-negotiable, such as during large promotional campaigns or time-sensitive communications.
    Preview results load in under 10 seconds, which dramatically reduces QA cycle
    time and allows teams to iterate quickly. Email on Acid functions as an essential
    quality assurance and analytics layer in a go-to-market stack, sitting alongside
    CRMs and email service providers rather than replacing them.
  - The tool complements existing delivery infrastructure by providing deep pre-send
    insight and post-send analytics, but it is not designed for building emails from
    scratch or managing subscriber lists. Its scope is focused entirely on rendering
    fidelity, deliverability diagnostics, and content optimization. Teams that need
    a dedicated QA step between email creation and deployment will find it a reliable
    addition to their workflow.
