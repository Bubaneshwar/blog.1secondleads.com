    score covering email content, authentication records, and sender configuration.

    actionable results.'

    to identify specific problems, such as missing SPF or DKIM records or content
    patterns that trigger spam filters, and correct them before the campaign goes
    out.

    tool. Its role is narrow and practical: give senders a clear view of their spam
