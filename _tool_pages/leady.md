  title: What is Leady?
  description:
  - Leady is a lead generation and customer intelligence platform that identifies
    the businesses visiting your website and enriches those visits with real-time
    company and contact data. By converting anonymous traffic into named prospects,
    it helps sales and marketing teams discover new leads and monitor existing customers'
    engagement without waiting for form submissions. Teams use Leady to deepen their
    understanding of buyer behavior and make faster, more informed decisions about
    outreach.
  - Leady is particularly effective for B2B companies, with a strong fit for SaaS
    businesses that want to track user behavior and run precisely targeted campaigns.
    Insights and qualified leads typically begin appearing within a day of setup,
    allowing sales and marketing teams to act on fresh data promptly rather than working
    from stale lists.
  - The platform integrates with over 750 CRM and marketing tools, making it straightforward
    to fold into an existing GTM stack. It supports email campaigns and data enrichment
    workflows, though it is not designed for direct consumer sales or large-scale
    eCommerce analytics. Its focus on B2B company profiles and buyer behavior makes
    it well suited to improving both conversion rates and customer retention.
