what_is:
  title: What is DealSignal?
  description:
  - DealSignal is a B2B data platform built for sales and marketing teams that need
    reliable, verified contact information at scale. It covers more than 1 billion
    global contacts and backs each record with a 97% accuracy rate and a 100% data
    guarantee. Teams rely on it to reach the right buyers, protect email deliverability,
    and drive higher conversion rates across their outreach programs.
  - The platform is well suited for building targeted decision-maker lists, enriching
    inbound form submissions, and improving the overall quality of CRM records. Users
    typically see pipeline growth accelerate shortly after onboarding because their
    outreach is reaching accurate contacts from day one. It is most valuable for professionals
    running account-based marketing or data-driven sales motions where contact accuracy
    is critical.
  - DealSignal connects with common CRM and marketing tools to automate data refreshes
    and keep records from going stale. It is focused on B2B contact and account data
    rather than consumer records, and it does not include a built-in sales engagement
    or sequencing layer. Teams looking for a precision data provider to complement
    their existing outreach infrastructure will find it a strong fit.
