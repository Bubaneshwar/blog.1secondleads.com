  title: What is Intentsify?
  description:
  - Intentsify is an AI-powered buyer intent platform designed to help B2B marketing
    and sales teams identify and engage buying groups at the right moment. It processes
    more than 1.1 trillion intent signals per month to produce persona-level insights,
    making it suitable for precisely targeted campaigns. The managed service model
    is a key differentiator, allowing customers to run and scale programs without
    integrating new self-serve software.
  - The platform is most effective when teams need to understand which accounts are
    actively in-market and want to accelerate sales cycles with higher campaign accuracy.
    Actionable insights for ad solutions, lead generation, and integrated campaigns
    become available as soon as customer data is connected, shortening the time from
    setup to results.
  - Intentsify complements existing CRM and marketing platforms by adding enriched
    intent data and audience intelligence rather than replacing them. It specializes
    in intent-driven identification and activation and requires integration with other
    sales or marketing tools to support full campaign execution. It is not a general-purpose
    marketing automation or standalone CRM solution.
