what_is:
  title: What is Cloudlead?
  description:
  - Cloudlead is a sales data provider that combines human research with automated
    tooling to deliver phone-validated contact information and accurate, up-to-date
    lead records. The dual approach, pairing human researchers with software validation,
    helps ensure data quality and relevance beyond what fully automated scraping can
    achieve, reducing time wasted pursuing invalid or outdated contacts.
  - The service is particularly effective for outbound sales, lead scoring, and cleaning
    existing contact databases. Enriched, verified records improve outreach accuracy
    and campaign ROI shortly after delivery, and the focus on decision-maker insights
    helps sales teams engage the right people within target accounts.
  - Cloudlead fits into the GTM stack as a data enrichment and cleansing layer that
    feeds more accurate information into CRMs and multi-channel outreach platforms.
    It is not a marketing automation or CRM tool itself and does not manage campaigns
    or track engagement. Its value is in improving the quality of the data that powers
    those downstream tools.
