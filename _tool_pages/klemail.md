  description:
  - Klemail is a straightforward email verification tool built to clean and maintain
    contact lists. It reduces bounce rates and helps senders avoid spam filters by
    checking each address for validity and flagging problematic entries. The service
    also applies automatic corrections where possible and delivers results with pre-built
    filters for easy review.
  - The tool suits marketers and businesses managing large email databases who need
    assurance that messages are reaching real recipients. Uploading a list in XLS,
    CSV, or TXT format kicks off a check that typically completes within minutes,
    making it practical for routine list hygiene without interrupting campaign workflows.
  - Within a go-to-market stack, Klemail fills the role of an email hygiene layer
    that complements CRM and email marketing platforms by ensuring the underlying
    contact data is accurate. It is not designed for sending emails or managing campaigns;
    its scope is limited to verifying and cleaning address lists quickly and securely.
