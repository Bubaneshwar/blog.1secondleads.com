what_is:
  title: What is LeadsRx?
  description:
  - LeadsRx is a marketing attribution platform built to measure and optimize performance
    across every channel a business uses. It helps teams understand precisely which
    marketing activities are generating sales by providing detailed, multi-touch attribution
    data. The platform's defining feature is its Universal Pixel, which captures 100%
    of digital inbound traffic without the need to retag individual ads, making deployment
    simple and the resulting data highly reliable.
  - LeadsRx is well suited to eCommerce, media companies, agencies, and enterprise
    marketing teams where mapping the full customer journey and attributing revenue
    accurately across multiple funnels is critical. Setup is straightforward, and
    teams generally gain actionable campaign insights within a short period after
    going live. The platform connects with tag managers and data platforms to enrich
    existing marketing datasets and fits naturally into a broader go-to-market technology
    stack.
  - LeadsRx does not manage CRM records or execute email campaigns. Its purpose is
    attribution clarity, offering a straightforward and comprehensive measurement
    layer for marketers who need to make confident, data-backed decisions about where
    to invest their budgets.
