what_is:
  title: What is EmailGuard?
  description:
  - EmailGuard is an email monitoring and protection tool built for improving deliverability
    and maintaining sender security. It sits within the email infrastructure layer,
    helping teams protect their domains, avoid spam filters, and preserve sender reputation
    over time. The platform consolidates SPF, DKIM, and DMARC authentication checks
    alongside blacklist status and inbox placement monitoring into a single interface.
  - The tool is particularly well suited for cold email outreach, email marketing
    teams, and sales organizations that depend on consistent inbox placement. Real-time
    insights make it possible to detect and resolve authentication or deliverability
    problems quickly, which reduces bounce rates, limits spam filtering, and helps
    campaigns perform better shortly after setup.
  - EmailGuard fits into the go-to-market stack as a backend health layer that complements
    CRMs and email service providers rather than replacing them. It does not handle
    campaign creation, contact enrichment, or direct sending, focusing entirely on
    the authentication and deliverability signals that determine whether messages
    reach the inbox. Keeping those signals healthy is what allows the rest of the
    email stack to work as intended.
