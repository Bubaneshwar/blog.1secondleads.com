what_is:
  title: What is Energy Leads?
  description:
  - EnergyLeads is a sales prospecting tool built specifically for the energy industry.
    It consolidates a single searchable database of more than 30,000 verified contacts
    spanning utilities, power generation, natural gas, and renewables across both
    US and EU markets. By targeting the energy vertical, it saves sales professionals
    the time and effort of assembling targeted contact lists from general-purpose
    databases that lack sector depth.
  - The tool is best suited for sellers, project developers, traders, and financiers
    active in energy projects who need accurate, role-filtered contact data quickly.
    Users can narrow results by job function or company type and export clean CSV
    files ready for immediate outreach, compressing the time from research to first
    contact. This speed and precision in pipeline building means fewer stale records
    and a higher proportion of reachable contacts.
  - Within a go-to-market stack, EnergyLeads operates as a lead sourcing and data
    enrichment resource that pairs well with a CRM and email outreach platform. It
    is not a CRM, marketing automation system, or outreach tool on its own, so it
    delivers the most value when combined with those systems. Its purpose is narrowly
    focused on verified contact discovery within the energy sector.
