  title: What is DitLead?
  description:
  - DitLead is a multi-channel outreach platform built for sales teams and marketers
    who need to run cold campaigns across LinkedIn, email, and phone simultaneously.
    It supports unlimited LinkedIn and email accounts, enabling teams to scale outreach
    well beyond what a single account allows. The platform is designed to send over
    1,000 leads daily while managing sending patterns to minimize the risk of blocks
    or blacklisting.
  - Outreach sequences can be fully automated, covering connection requests, follow-up
    messages, and email campaigns in a single workflow. The built-in AI personalization
    layer helps tailor each message to the individual prospect, which improves response
    rates compared to generic templates. DitLead functions as a sales engagement CRM
    within the go-to-market stack and integrates with major email providers including
    Gmail, Outlook, and AWS SES for reliable delivery.
  - The platform is focused on cold outreach and lead management rather than complex
    CRM workflows or deep data enrichment. Teams that need a single tool to coordinate
    and scale multi-channel prospecting will find it fits naturally into their outbound
    process, while those looking for advanced pipeline analytics or inbound lead handling
    will need complementary tools.
