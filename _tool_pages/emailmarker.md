  description:
  - Emailmarker is an email verification and list-cleaning service built to improve
    digital marketing results by removing addresses that hurt deliverability. It filters
    out invalid, fraudulent, and duplicate emails so campaigns reach real inboxes
    rather than bouncing or triggering spam complaints. The service delivers 97% accuracy
    in real time, offers 150 free verifications for new accounts, and starts at $3
    for 1,000 email checks, making it accessible for teams of all sizes.
  - Emailmarker is most valuable when you need to clean large lists before bulk sends,
    cut bounce rates, or lift open rates on outreach campaigns. Results are returned
    promptly, so senders can act on improved data right away. The tool also exposes
    a real-time verification API that developers can embed directly into websites
    and apps, allowing new addresses to be validated at the point of capture rather
    than in post-collection batches.
  - The platform is purpose-built for list hygiene and address verification rather
    than full contact or customer management. It works best as a pre-import step before
    loading contacts into a CRM or email platform, not as a standalone outreach or
    marketing tool. Teams that combine Emailmarker with their existing sending infrastructure
    gain a cleaner database and more consistent campaign performance over time.
