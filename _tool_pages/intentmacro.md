  title: What is IntentMacro?
  description:
  - IntentMacro is a B2B sales and marketing intelligence tool built to identify company-level
    buying intent and map individual user interests within target accounts. It tracks
    intent signals to reveal real-time buying activity and demand patterns across
    the market. The platform uses AI and machine learning to deliver accurate intent
    data and connect content consumption to specific contacts.
  - It is best suited for sales and marketing teams that want to prioritize outreach
    based on fresh, verified buying signals rather than static firmographic data.
    The database is continuously updated to reflect current buyer behavior, so teams
    can act on timely information and uncover demand within entire buying committees
    rather than just individual contacts.
  - Within a go-to-market stack, IntentMacro serves as an intelligence layer that
    enriches CRM and email workflows with targeted intent data. It does not handle
    direct communication or campaign execution; its role is to sharpen targeting and
    improve lead quality before outreach begins.
