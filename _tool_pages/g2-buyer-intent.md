  title: What is G2 Buyer Intent?
  description:
  - G2 Buyer Intent is a sales intelligence tool that helps businesses identify and
    connect with buyers who are actively evaluating software. It belongs to the lead
    generation and sales intelligence category. What distinguishes it is access to
    verified, real-time intent data sourced directly from G2's platform, so teams
    can target prospects who are already in a buying motion rather than relying on
    broad outreach.
  - The tool is most valuable when sales efficiency matters and teams want to concentrate
    effort on accounts showing clear purchase signals. Because intent data flows in
    real time as buyers research your product or competing solutions, teams can act
    immediately and personalize outreach to match the prospect's stage. This focus
    on mid- and bottom-funnel signals helps shorten sales cycles and improve conversion
    rates.
  - G2 Buyer Intent connects with CRM and marketing platforms including Salesforce,
    HubSpot, and Marketo, enabling automated workflows and more accurate lead scoring.
    It is not designed for cold lead generation or broad market research; its strength
    is delivering high-confidence signals for accounts already engaged in an active
    evaluation.
