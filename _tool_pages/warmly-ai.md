  description:
  - Warmly is a signal-based outbound tool that focuses on prospects already showing
    interest. It de-anonymizes website traffic to show which companies and people
    are visiting, then combines that with intent and CRM data so reps reach out when
    an account is in-market.
  - 'It is most useful for turning anonymous site visits into timely conversations:
    surfacing warm accounts, alerting reps, and triggering engagement across chat,
    email, and LinkedIn. The aim is to prioritize accounts that are likely to respond
    rather than cold lists.'
  - Warmly connects to CRMs, enrichment, and outreach tools to fit a go-to-market
    stack. It concentrates on visitor identification, intent, and engagement rather
    than acting as a full CRM or marketing-automation suite.
