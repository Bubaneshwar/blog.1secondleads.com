what_is:
  title: What is LeadGenius?
  description:
  - LeadGenius is a B2B contact intelligence platform designed for sales and marketing
    teams that need fresh, accurate, and compliant prospect data. Rather than relying
    on a static database, it delivers live, on-demand contact intelligence by combining
    AI-powered sourcing with human verification. This approach produces data that
    meets GDPR and CCPA requirements and covers both global markets and niche segments
    that typical contact databases rarely include.
  - The platform is particularly effective for teams tackling incomplete contact records,
    expanding into new geographies, or targeting buying committees across multiple
    stakeholders. Some clients have reported closing new deals within 14 days of adopting
    the tool, owing to the quality and specificity of the contacts provided. LeadGenius
    also surfaces insights into full buying committees, helping teams engage every
    key decision-maker rather than a single contact per account.
  - LeadGenius connects with CRM and marketing automation platforms so enriched data
    flows directly into existing workflows, keeping records current and actionable.
    It operates as a real-time data source and precision lead generator rather than
    a standalone sales engagement or marketing automation platform, making it a complement
    to the broader GTM tech stack rather than a replacement for it.
