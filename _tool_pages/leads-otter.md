what_is:
  title: What is Leads Otter?
  description:
  - Leads Otter is a B2B sales prospecting tool that converts job postings into qualified
    leads by treating hiring activity as a reliable indicator of buying potential.
    It fits within lead generation and sales prospecting workflows, monitoring data
    from more than 60 countries and over 100,000 job boards with updates every 8 to
    12 hours. Because companies that are actively hiring are often simultaneously
    evaluating new vendors and tools, this real-time hiring data allows sales teams
    to reach prospects at a moment when they are more likely to be receptive.
  - The platform excels at identifying companies that are growing headcount, replacing
    key roles, or expanding into new regions. Leads become actionable as soon as hiring
    signals are detected, enabling timely outreach before the opportunity cools or
    competitors move in. Contacts and signals can be exported to a CRM or other sales
    systems via CSV, with webhook delivery available as an upcoming capability.
  - Leads Otter is designed to complement existing sales data sources by adding a
    hiring-driven signal layer, not to replace a full CRM or contact database. It
    does not cover general marketing automation; its role is specific to surfacing
    hiring-driven sales opportunities. Teams looking to add a fresh, frequently updated
    source of relevant leads will find it a focused and easy-to-adopt addition to
    their prospecting workflow.
