what_is:
  title: What is Clay?
  description:
  - Clay is a go-to-market automation tool that helps teams build and enrich lead
    lists from over 100 data sources in one workflow. It runs waterfall enrichment
    and AI research agents to fill in contact details, company data, and buying signals,
    keeping a CRM accurate without manual research.
  - Teams reach for Clay when they need targeted prospect lists, inbound lead enrichment,
    or signal-based outbound at scale. It connects to CRMs, email sequencers, and
    data warehouses so enriched data flows into the tools reps already use.
  - Clay is built for data and outreach workflows rather than full marketing campaign
    management or deep analytics, so it usually sits alongside the rest of a GTM stack.
