what_is:
  title: What is RB2B?
  description:
  - RB2B is a website visitor identification tool that resolves traffic to person-level
    identity for US visitors and company-level identity more broadly. It turns anonymous
    visits into named leads you can follow up on, with real-time data pushed to tools
    like Slack and your CRM.
  - It is most useful for surfacing high-value leads filtered by attributes like company
    size, revenue, or location. Setup takes minutes, and leads start appearing in
    Slack almost immediately, so teams can act on intent without waiting.
  - RB2B integrates with many apps including Salesforce, HubSpot, and Slack, fitting
    the GTM stack for enrichment and outreach. Person-level identification is US-focused
    due to privacy rules like GDPR, so its sweet spot is fast US lead identification.
