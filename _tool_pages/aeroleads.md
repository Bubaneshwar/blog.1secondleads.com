  title: What is AeroLeads?
  description:
  - AeroLeads is a B2B data and contact discovery platform that locates verified emails,
    phone numbers, and company information for sales and marketing teams. Drawing
    on a database of 750 million profiles, it applies AI-driven validation to return
    accurate, real-time contact records rather than stale bulk exports.
  - The tool is particularly useful for building targeted prospect lists, enriching
    existing contact records, or sourcing leads from LinkedIn URLs and CSV uploads.
    Results are typically available within minutes, letting teams launch outreach
    campaigns without the delays associated with manual research or slow data providers.
  - AeroLeads sits in the GTM stack as a data enrichment and prospecting layer, syncing
    verified contacts directly into CRMs such as HubSpot and Salesforce. It is focused
    exclusively on clean B2B lead data and does not offer consumer data, social media
    management, or standalone CRM functionality.
