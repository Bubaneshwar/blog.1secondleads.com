what_is:
  title: What is LeadPost?
  description:
  - LeadPost is a website visitor identification tool built for businesses and agencies
    that want to capture leads from traffic that would otherwise leave without any
    trace. Instead of returning IP ranges or company guesses, it delivers real contact
    details, including names, email addresses, and physical addresses, for up to 40%
    of site visitors. This significantly increases the pool of reachable prospects
    without requiring any changes to the existing site beyond one line of code.
  - LeadPost is well suited to B2B lead routing, B2C targeting, and agency setups
    managing multiple clients. Because the implementation is minimal, qualified leads
    start appearing quickly after the snippet goes live, giving marketers a fast path
    to actionable data. Teams that want to act on visitor behavior without waiting
    weeks for form-fill accumulation find it particularly practical.
  - 'The tool fits into broader marketing stacks by supporting data enrichment, CRM
    lead routing, and built-in retargeting across email, social, display, and direct
    mail channels. It is not a full marketing automation platform and does not rely
    on third-party cookies. Its scope is deliberately narrow: identify visitors, enrich
    the resulting contact data, and feed that data into downstream channels while
    remaining compliant with applicable privacy regulations.'
