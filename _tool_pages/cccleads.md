  title: What is CCCLeads?
  description:
  - CCCLeads is a lead database tool built specifically for businesses that sell to
    coaches, consultants, and course creators. It delivers verified contact records
    with more than 12 data points per entry, including confirmed email addresses,
    so users can start outreach without spending time cleaning or validating raw data.
    One-click export keeps the process simple and fast.
  - The platform is a strong fit for small agencies and individual professionals who
    need a reliable pool of pre-verified leads for cold email outreach and client
    acquisition. Because the leads are ready to export immediately after a search,
    teams can move from prospecting to campaign launch in a very short time, which
    is valuable when pipeline velocity matters.
  - CCCLeads functions as a dedicated data source within the GTM stack, feeding clean
    contact information into email sequences or CRM systems. It is not an email marketing
    platform or a full CRM, so it works best alongside separate tools that handle
    campaign execution, engagement tracking, and relationship management.
