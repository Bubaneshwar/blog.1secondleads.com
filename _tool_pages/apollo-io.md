  description:
  - Apollo.io is a sales intelligence and engagement platform built for outbound teams.
    It pairs a contact and company database of more than 275 million records with
    email finding, phone numbers, and CRM enrichment, so reps can build targeted lists
    without juggling separate data tools.
  - Teams use Apollo to prospect, verify contact data, and run email and call sequences,
    then sync everything back to their CRM. It fits sales development and full-cycle
    reps who want lead data and outreach in a single workflow.
  - Apollo is not a full marketing automation suite or a replacement for a primary
    CRM, but it works alongside tools like HubSpot and Salesforce to keep pipeline
    data current and outreach moving.
