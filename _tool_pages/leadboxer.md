what_is:
  title: What is LeadBoxer?
  description:
  - LeadBoxer is a lead generation and qualification platform that helps businesses
    identify the people and companies engaging with their digital properties. It converts
    anonymous visitor activity into sales-ready leads through automated scoring and
    detailed company profiling, giving revenue teams a clearer picture of who is interested
    and how engaged they are.
  - The platform suits sales and marketing teams that need visitor identification,
    lead scoring, and lead management consolidated in one place. Value is visible
    quickly after implementation, with new lead insights typically surfacing as soon
    as the tracking is live. Faster identification of high-potential prospects translates
    directly into more targeted campaign activity and better use of sales capacity.
  - LeadBoxer connects with CRM systems, email platforms, and data enrichment tools
    to centralize lead records and automate handoff workflows. It is not intended
    to replace a full CRM or to handle complex sales process automation. Its core
    strength is filling the lead intelligence and qualification gap that often exists
    between marketing activity and sales follow-up.
