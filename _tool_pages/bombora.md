what_is:
  title: What is Bombora?
  description:
  - Bombora is a B2B intent-data provider. It aggregates content-consumption signals
    from a large cooperative of business sites and analyzes them to show which accounts
    are researching topics related to what you sell, so teams can engage at the right
    moment.
  - It is most useful for identifying in-market accounts, reducing churn risk, and
    tailoring messaging to what an audience is actually reading. Because data can
    be activated in real time, teams can prioritize and personalize campaigns quickly.
  - Bombora integrates with CRM, marketing automation, and ad platforms like HubSpot,
    Salesforce, and LinkedIn. It is explicitly a data provider rather than a standalone
    platform, so it relies on your existing systems to act on its signals.
