  description:
  - ColdClicks is an AI-powered cold email personalization tool built for sales teams
    and lead generation agencies. It generates unique email copy for each prospect
    by drawing on real-time data from more than 50 sources, combining AI-generated,
    dynamic, and static content within a single template. The result is highly relevant
    messages that increase reply rates while staying out of spam folders.
  - The tool is most effective for large-scale cold email campaigns where speed and
    personalization both matter. Users upload their lead list, map the relevant fields,
    and ColdClicks produces tailored emails immediately, saving hours of manual writing.
    Positive reply rates improve without additional effort from the sender.
  - ColdClicks connects with widely used CRMs and email platforms including Lemlist,
    Apollo, and Smartlead, slotting neatly into existing sales workflows. It is not
    a full CRM or email sending platform; its purpose is specifically to enrich lead
    data and personalize outreach copy, so a separate tool is still needed to deliver
    the campaigns.
