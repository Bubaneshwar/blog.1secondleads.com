what_is:
  title: What is Gmass?
  description:
  - GMass is a cold email and mail-merge tool built into Gmail. It turns the Gmail
    compose window into a campaign sender, personalizing messages from Google Sheets
    and handling the sending limits that normally cap outreach from a regular inbox.
  - It is handy for newsletters, cold email campaigns, and personalized announcements,
    with automated follow-ups and list building. Because it lives in Gmail, there
    is little to learn and campaigns can go out almost immediately after setup.
  - GMass fits the stack as a Gmail and Google Sheets sending extension rather than
    a CRM or enrichment tool. It is best used purely for mass and personalized emailing
    inside Gmail.
