what_is:
  title: What is Hublead?
  description:
  - Hublead is a Chrome extension that connects LinkedIn with HubSpot CRM to eliminate
    manual data entry and keep contact records accurate. Sales teams can add LinkedIn
    contacts to HubSpot and sync LinkedIn activity in one click, accelerating prospecting
    and maintaining data quality without switching between tools.
  - It is best suited for sales reps, managers, founders, and RevOps teams who want
    faster outreach and clearer visibility into LinkedIn-sourced activity. The time
    savings are immediate, with reps spending less time on administrative work and
    managers gaining access to more reliable activity data from day one.
  - Hublead serves as a focused LinkedIn-to-HubSpot integration layer for CRM enrichment
    and activity tracking. It does not send LinkedIn messages from HubSpot and does
    not currently support LinkedIn Recruiter or the Safari browser. Its strength is
    reliable contact syncing and data enrichment rather than outbound messaging.
