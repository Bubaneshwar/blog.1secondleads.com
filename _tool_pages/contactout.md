what_is:
  title: What is ContactOut?
  description:
  - ContactOut is a contact data platform designed to locate emails and phone numbers
    for professionals. It is trusted by 1.4 million recruiters and sales representatives
    and provides access to 300 million verified professional contacts with 99% data
    confidence. The database spans 800 million profiles and includes more than 20
    data points per contact, with records updated hourly to maintain accuracy.
  - The tool is most valuable for sales prospecting and recruitment, where users need
    to build precise, targeted lists quickly. Through its Chrome extension, prospects
    can be added to email campaigns in a single click. It is well suited to situations
    requiring verified contacts with detailed attributes such as job title, company
    size, and technologies used. ContactOut plugs into CRMs and email outreach platforms
    as a contact enrichment and email-finding layer.
  - It is not built for full CRM management or marketing automation. Its strength
    is straightforward, reliable contact discovery, making it the right fit for teams
    that need accurate contact data without complex additional workflows.
