  - Anymail Finder is an email lookup tool focused on accuracy and cost efficiency.
    It searches for professional email addresses and verifies each result before delivery,
    targeting a valid-send rate above 97%. The pay-for-verified model means users
    are charged only when an email passes verification, and addresses flagged as risky
    or not found carry no cost.
  - The tool serves sales, marketing, HR, and business development professionals who
    need to reach specific contacts quickly. Searches can be run by full name, company
    name, domain, or by targeting decision-makers within an organization, and results
    are typically returned almost instantly. Bulk search capabilities, a browser extension,
    and a developer API help teams handle larger prospecting volumes without slowing
    down their pipeline.
  - 'Anymail Finder integrates with CRMs, email platforms, spreadsheets, and automation
    tools including Zapier and Make.com, making it a practical enrichment layer within
    an outbound GTM stack. Its scope is intentionally focused: it finds and verifies
    email addresses efficiently rather than functioning as a full contact database
