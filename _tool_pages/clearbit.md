what_is:
  title: What is Clearbit?
  description:
  - Clearbit is a B2B data tool that gives sales and marketing teams clean, enriched
    information on leads, contacts, and accounts. It combines public web data with
    proprietary sources and AI to keep records accurate, with broad coverage and detailed
    firmographic classification.
  - It is most useful for lead scoring, routing, and identifying high-fit prospects.
    Clearbit can reveal buying intent from anonymous website traffic and shorten forms
    by auto-filling known details, which helps lift conversion. Because data updates
    in real time, enrichment takes effect as soon as it is connected.
  - Clearbit acts as the data foundation in a go-to-market stack, complementing CRMs
    and marketing tools. It is not a CRM or an outreach platform on its own; it supplies
    the data that other tools use to run campaigns and communications.
