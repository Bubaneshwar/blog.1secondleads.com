  title: What is MailerCheck?
  description:
  - MailerCheck is an email verification and deliverability testing platform with
    over 12 years of industry experience behind it. It helps businesses improve inbox
    placement and open rates by verifying that email addresses are active and checking
    that message content does not trigger spam filters. Teams rely on its track record
    for accuracy when maintaining large contact databases.
  - 'The platform covers the full pre-send quality cycle: cleaning lists of invalid
    addresses, scoring content against common spam rules, predicting whether messages
    will reach the inbox or the junk folder, and monitoring sending domains for blocklist
    appearances. Each check returns actionable data within seconds, keeping list maintenance
    and campaign preparation on a fast timeline.'
  - MailerCheck integrates with widely used email platforms such as Mailchimp and
    ActiveCampaign, slotting in as a verification and monitoring layer alongside CRM
    and sending tools. It is not a list-building or campaign-creation product; it
    is focused on ensuring that whatever emails a team sends have the best possible
    chance of reaching the intended inbox.
