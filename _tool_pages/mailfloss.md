  title: What is Mailfloss?
  description:
  - Mailfloss is an automated email verification tool that cleans contact lists on
    a daily schedule without requiring manual intervention. It removes invalid addresses
    before they accumulate, prevents emails from drifting into spam, and automatically
    corrects common typos in addresses tied to major providers like Gmail and Yahoo.
    Initial setup takes around 60 seconds, after which the cleaning process runs on
    its own.
  - Marketers, agencies, and businesses that send regular email campaigns benefit
    most from having a continuously maintained list. Because invalid contacts are
    removed on a daily basis rather than in periodic manual audits, deliverability
    stays high over time and ROI improves as more messages reach real recipients.
  - Mailfloss connects to more than 40 email service providers including Mailchimp
    and HubSpot, making it straightforward to add to an existing marketing or CRM
    stack. Its scope is deliberately narrow, focused on verification and ongoing list
    hygiene rather than segmentation, enrichment, or campaign management, which makes
    it a practical, low-maintenance addition for any team that depends on email.
