# 1SecondLeads Brand Kit

This document serves as the technical reference for any AI agent tasked with creating designs, websites, or marketing materials for **1SecondLeads**.

---

## 🎨 Color Palette
Use these specific HEX codes for all CSS styling.

- **Deep Black (Primary Background):** `#000000`
- **Electric Purple (Action/Accent/Brand):** `#673ae4`
- **Off-Black (Secondary UI/Cards):** `#0a0a0a`
- **Dark Gray (Borders/Dividers):** `#222222`
- **Titanium White (Primary Text):** `#ffffff`
- **Subtle Gray (Secondary Text):** `#888888`

---

## 🖋️ Typography
- **Primary Font:** `Plus Jakarta Sans`
- **Highlight Font:** `Instrument Serif` (Use exclusively to highlight certain specific words in headings to give a premium, high-converting look)
- **CSS Fallbacks:** `'Plus Jakarta Sans', sans-serif` and `'Instrument Serif', serif`
- **Styling Rule:** Use high-weight/bold Plus Jakarta Sans fonts for the main value propositions to make them punchy. Use the Instrument Serif font (often italicized alongside the purple accent color) for core differentiator words.

---

## 🚀 Core Messaging & Tone
- **Tone:** Direct, high-value, B2B, performance-driven.
- **Main Headline Ideas:** 
  - More Qualified Appointments.
  - Scale your outbound on performance.
  - We build sales pipelines. You get high-intent clients.
- **Core Differentiator:** Zero Monthly Commitment.
- **Target Audience:** Marketing Agencies & Software Solutions.
- **Subheadline:** We help marketing agencies build and scale outbound sales pipelines that consistently attract high-intent clients.

---

## 🖼️ Visual Identity
- **Logo Style:** Minimalist, often just "1SL" in a bold, clean white font.
- **Aesthetic:** Modern "dark mode" SaaS or Framer-style. High-contrast text on black backgrounds.
- **Design Elements:** Use vibrant electric purple `#673ae4` for radial background glows, subtle border highlights, and text accents. Avoid clutter; embrace negative space.
